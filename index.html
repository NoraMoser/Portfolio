About Nora:

As a holder of a Masters Information Science, most of my professional experience has been spent as a school librarian; but I began to realize that I enjoyed the technological side of information science more than the literature side. And so when I invited a professional coder to teach programming to a class of 8th grade girls, I discovered that I had a knack for the subject, and began to search for ways to develop my interest and transition it into a career. Developer friends confirmed that Nashville Software School was the best place to pursue an education as a developer, and since enrolling at NSS I have learned that I not only have a knack for coding - I also have a passion. At NSS I have become a full stack apprentice developer, and I am thrilled for the opportunity to continue growing and learning as a professional developer..
